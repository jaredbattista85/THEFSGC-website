Our Flagship IP: Fantasy+

Fantasy+ is a new, free-to-play fantasy football app built for maximum accessibility and unlimited community scale. It offers a fast, incredibly fun, and low-commitment way to play while competing in a massive, app-wide, season-long tournament.

Infinite Scaling: Draft your favorite marquee stars at every position with zero draft-day constraints. Opponents can pick the same players, allowing for infinite group sizes where the best overall combination wins.

NFL Fanbase Leagues: Designed for all players, you can instantly join public leagues dedicated entirely to your favorite NFL team's community.
